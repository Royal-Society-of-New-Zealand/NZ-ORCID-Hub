Welcome To The NZ ORCID Hub

The Hub connects New Zealand ORCID Consortium member organisations with their researchers in ORCID.

Institutions can use the Hub to connect (with permission) to their staff and students’ ORCID records.
Staff and students can use the hub to give permission to their institution to access their ORCID records.

For more information on the Hub's background and development please go to the Hub page of the Royal Society Te Apārangi’s website.
